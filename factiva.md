## Media Keyword Classification 

IPCC relevance in the media was assessed through a keyword-based search.
The following permutations were used to identify references to the IPCC
and to individual Working Groups.

| Category | Keywords / Search Strings |
|---------|---------------------------|
| **IPCC (overall)** | `(IPCC)` OR `(Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change)` |
| **Working Group I** | `(IPCC AND Working Group I)` OR `(IPCC AND WGI)` OR `(IPCC AND WG1)` OR `(Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change AND WGI)` OR `(Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change AND Working Group I)` OR `(Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change AND Working Group 1)` OR `(IPCC AND Working Group 1)` OR `(IPCC AND Working GroupI)` |
| **Working Group II** | `(IPCC AND Working Group II)` OR `(IPCC AND WGII)` OR `(IPCC AND WG2)` OR `(Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change AND WGII)` OR `(Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change AND Working Group II)` OR `(Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change AND Working Group 2)` OR `(IPCC AND Working Group 2)` OR `(IPCC AND Working Group II)` |
| **Working Group III** | `(IPCC AND Working Group III)` OR `(IPCC AND WGIII)` OR `(IPCC AND WG3)` OR `(Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change AND WGIII)` OR `(Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change AND Working Group III)` OR `(Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change AND Working Group 3)` OR `(IPCC AND Working Group 3)` OR `(IPCC AND Working Group III)` |
